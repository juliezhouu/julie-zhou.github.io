False Alert Management

Initial detection system triggered excessive false positives when users made natural movements or leaned forward intentionally. Implemented a time-based threshold system with consecutive frame validation to distinguish between momentary position changes and sustained poor posture.

üîç

Lighting Variability

Pose detection accuracy degraded significantly in low-light or backlit conditions. Developed adaptive preprocessing filters and confidence score thresholds that adjust based on ambient lighting conditions to maintain reliable tracking.

‚ö°

Performance Optimization

Early versions experienced frame rate drops on lower-end hardware. Resolved through strategic frame skipping, model complexity reduction, and implementing efficient caching mechanisms to maintain 30 FPS on standard laptops.
